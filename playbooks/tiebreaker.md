# 10-Minute Tie-Breaker Mode

## Roles
- Captain: reads challenge, assigns the only target, and submits final answer.
- Solver: performs the main technical path.
- Verifier: checks assumptions, validates flag format, and catches case-sensitivity mistakes.
- Support: opens references, Quirk, calculators, and AI prompts on demand.

## Minute Plan
- 0-1: identify category, inputs, output format, and likely trick.
- 1-3: generate two plausible solving paths; pick the fastest with visible evidence.
- 3-7: solve, calculate, script, or simulate.
- 7-8: verifier independently checks flag and evidence.
- 8-9: captain submits if high confidence.
- 9-10: if wrong, try only one evidence-backed alternate.

## Rule
- No speculative mass submissions. One clean wrong answer is better than five noisy guesses.
