# CTFd Safety Rules

## Allowed
- Solve challenge instances and downloaded artifacts.
- Use AI for challenge understanding, scripts, summaries, and solution derivation.
- Use local tooling, Quirk, browser developer tools on challenge pages, and read-only CTFd API checks.
- Use low-rate, read-only crawling on challenge URLs that are explicitly in scope.

## Not Allowed
- Attacking, disrupting, scanning, or stress-testing CTFd infrastructure.
- Denial-of-service activity or automated abuse.
- Exploiting platform vulnerabilities outside intended challenge scope.
- Automated flag brute force or high-rate submission loops.
- Broad automated scanning of the CTFd platform, venue network, or third-party sites outside challenge scope.
- Sharing flags, answers, or private challenge information outside the team.

## Team Rule
- The captain submits flags manually in CTFd.
- The toolkit may validate format and fetch read-only information, but does not auto-submit flags.
- If a challenge instruction is unclear, ask a proctor for clarification without requesting hints.
